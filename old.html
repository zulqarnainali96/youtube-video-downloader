Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our YouTube downloader service.

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Downloading YouTube videos is against YouTube's Terms of Service. However, it is legal for personal use in many jurisdictions. We recommend using downloaded videos only for personal, offline viewing and always respecting copyright laws.

Do I need to install software to use this service?

No, our YouTube downloader is completely web-based. You don't need to install any software or browser extensions. Just visit our website and start downloading videos directly from your browser.

What video formats do you support?

We support multiple formats including MP4 (1080p, 720p, 480p) for video downloads and MP3 for audio-only downloads. The available formats depend on the original video's quality settings.

Is there a limit to how many videos I can download?

No, there are no limits on the number of videos you can download. You can download as many videos as you want, whenever you want.

How long does it take to download a video?

Download times depend on your internet connection speed and the size of the video. Our servers are optimized for fast downloads, so it should be as fast as your connection allows.

Do you store downloaded videos on your servers?

No, we don't store any downloaded videos on our servers. Videos are processed in real-time and sent directly to your browser. We also don't keep logs of your download activity.

Can I download videos on my mobile device?

Yes, our service works on all modern mobile devices including smartphones and tablets. Simply visit our website using your mobile browser and download videos just like you would on a desktop.

Why isn't the video downloading?

If you're having trouble downloading a video, please try the following:

  1. Ensure you've entered the correct YouTube URL
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Try a different format
  4. Clear your browser cache and try again

If problems persist, please contact our support team.
